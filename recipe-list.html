Traditional Afghan Food

Manto

Afghan mantu is perhaps the most popular dish from Afghanistan
. It is a meat (Beef/Lamb) stuffed dumpling that
is steamed in a multilayer steamer.
Mantu is served on special occasions and
large gatherings. In Afghanistan, cooked mantu is
often sold at markets and busy streets
much like hotdog stands. Mantu can be
served as a main dish or a side dish.
